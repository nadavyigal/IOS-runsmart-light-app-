RunSmart 1.0.9 makes your first days with the app clearer and more trustworthy, from sign-in through your first plan.

What's new:
- Your plan builds in front of you: Today and Plan show progress while your coach works, with a clear retry if anything fails
- Onboarding polish: a Back button, clearer steps, and your goal is always your explicit choice
- Rest days now answer "what should I do today?" with concrete recovery guidance
- Week numbers, streaks, distances, and workout intensity now match everywhere they appear
- Terms and Privacy open inside the app; clearer messages when sign-in or Apple Health connection fails
- Accurate workout breakdowns (interval counts parsed from your actual workout)
