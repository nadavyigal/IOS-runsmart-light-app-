RunSmart 1.1.3 brings the route tools fully to life and keeps your saved routes safe.

What's new:
- Build a route and start a run from it in one tap with the new Use This Route button
- Open any saved or benchmark route to see its details, make it a benchmark, and track your stats over time
- Your saved routes and benchmarks now sync to your account, so they're no longer lost when you reinstall the app
- Route screens open full-height so you can see every route at a glance
