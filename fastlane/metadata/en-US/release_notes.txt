RunSmart 1.0.8 improves run recording reliability, adds live kilometer splits and lock-screen run status, and makes Apple Health part of onboarding with automatic workout import.

What's new:
- Connect Apple Health during onboarding; workouts import right after you connect
- Today's activity card shows steps and calories from Apple Health
- More reliable GPS recording, finish/discard flows, and run controls on all iPhone sizes
- Live per-km splits while you run; moving time labels; better post-run summaries
- Lock-screen and Dynamic Island run status while recording (supported devices)
