# App Store Standards

- Do not overclaim AI coaching, fitness outcomes, or health benefits.
- Make subscription, credits, and paid features clear before purchase.
- Keep screenshots and metadata aligned with the actual app.
- Ensure privacy labels match real data collection.
- Include support and privacy policy URLs before submission.
- Provide reviewer notes and demo credentials when needed.
- Remove placeholder names, debug controls, and stale product branding.
- Verify capabilities, entitlements, usage descriptions, version, build number, and archive validation before upload.
