# RunSmart — Audience

Last synced from: `RunSmart iOS/docs/product-strategy-2026-05.md` (2026-05-24)

## Primary Segments

### The Rookie (28–45)

- Trigger: new or returning runner — signed up for a 5K, 10K, half, or full marathon, or returning after a long break or injury
- Fitness: runs 1–3x/week, no wearable or basic Apple Watch
- History: tried NRC or a free training plan; got injured or lost motivation by week 3
- Pains: doesn't know if the plan is calibrated to their actual fitness; missed sessions feel like failure; doesn't want to re-injure
- Workarounds: free PDFs, Couch to 5K, asking ChatGPT
- Words they use: "training plan", "couch to 5K", "first race", "should I do this run", "easy run pace", "beginner running plan"
- Win condition: hits week 2 and thinks "this coach actually knows me"

### The Striver (25–40) with a wearable

- Trigger: intermediate runner with Garmin or Apple Watch; churned from Runna because the plan didn't adapt when life got in the way
- Fitness: runs 3–5x/week; generates data they don't fully trust or act on
- Pains: plateauing; life disrupts plan; wants the data to mean something; coach is too expensive
- Workarounds: self-coaching, manual plan-dragging in Runna, asking a friend
- Words they use: "adaptive plan", "RPE", "deload", "marathon plan", "Garmin training", "Strava analysis", "HRV"
- Win condition: replaces manual plan-dragging with AI that makes the adjustment for them

## Acquisition Wedge

Runners who have churned from Runna, specifically citing injury or plan rigidity. Best reached via comparison content ("RunSmart vs Runna"), SEO on "adaptive training plan" + "AI running coach for beginners", and the App Store listing.

## Anti-Segments

- Elite or sub-elite athletes — they need a human coach
- Pure walkers — not a fit yet
- Users who want a pure social fitness app
- Android users — out of scope for 2026

## Where To Reach Each

| Segment | Best Channels |
|---|---|
| The Rookie | ASO, SEO ("beginner 5k plan", "couch to 5k AI", "first marathon training"), beginner-focused landing pages, partnerships with newcomer-focused coaches |
| The Striver | Garmin ecosystem content, "return to running after injury" SEO, Runna comparison page, Strava community (observation only) |
