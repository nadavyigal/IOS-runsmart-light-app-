# RunSmart iOS Product Vision

RunSmart iOS is a daily running companion that helps runners understand what to do today, why it matters, and how to adjust when life or recovery changes the plan.

## Principles
- Make the next action obvious.
- Use AI coaching to clarify, not decorate.
- Favor trust, calm, and practical guidance over dense dashboards.
- Keep screens focused and mobile-first.
- Make progress, readiness, and recovery easy to understand.
- Respect runner privacy and health data sensitivity.

## Core Experience
- Today: next workout, readiness, recovery cue, recent activity, and one clear action.
- Run: start, track, pause, finish, and review with minimal friction.
- Plan: weekly structure, upcoming sessions, goal progress, and adjustment reasons.
- Coach: concise answers and personalized guidance based on app data.
- Profile: runner preferences, goal, integrations, privacy, subscription/account settings.

## Non-Goals
- Copying the web app screen-for-screen.
- Overloading the Today screen with every metric.
- Shipping AI features that feel generic or unverifiable.
- Adding integrations before permission, privacy, and failure states are clear.
