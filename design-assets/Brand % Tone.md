# RunSmart Brand & Tone Guide

## Brand Position

RunSmart is a premium AI running coach for runners who want a plan that feels personal, adaptive, and easy to trust. The iOS app should feel native, fast, dark-first, and lighter than the web experience.

## Visual Identity

- Primary accent: electric lime / neon yellow-green.
- Background: near black.
- Cards: deep black, graphite, and dark green-black surfaces.
- Text: white primary text with accessible soft gray secondary text.
- Supporting states: lime for progress and success, amber for warning, cool blue or muted green for recovery, soft red for errors.
- Logo direction: centered RS mark, athletic forward motion, no small text inside app icons.
- App icon direction: black rounded-square background, electric lime RS mark, subtle glow, readable at small size.

## Product Feel

- Premium, athletic, calm, and modern.
- Native SwiftUI, not a direct copy of the web app.
- Dashboard-first: Today is the daily hub.
- Keep screens scannable and uncluttered.
- Use lime sparingly for intent, progress, and primary action.
- Avoid heavy marketing language inside the app.

## Voice & Tone

- Friendly coach, not corporate trainer.
- Clear, direct, supportive, and specific.
- Prefer short action-oriented guidance.
- Make adaptive decisions explainable without sounding technical.
- Encourage consistency and recovery as much as speed.

## Copy Examples

- "Your coach is ready when you are."
- "Start smooth. We can adjust after the first kilometer."
- "Recovery is part of the plan today."
- "Your 21-Day Rookie Challenge is active."
- "Garmin and HealthKit can improve coaching context when connected."

## Core Screens

- Today: daily workout or rest state, weekly progress, quick actions, coach insight.
- Plan: full plan overview, week sections, current and completed workout highlights.
- Record Run: sport-watch metrics, clear record/pause/resume/finish controls, route area.
- Coach Chat: calm AI coach, suggested questions, context-aware answers.
- Onboarding: five steps: goal, experience, schedule, privacy, confirmation.
- Profile: stats, connected devices, reminders, privacy, subscription/status placeholders.

## Manual Export Note

The implementation includes cropped placeholder production assets in the asset catalog. For App Store quality, export a clean vector/PDF or 1024x1024 PNG of the dark icon with:

- no transparency
- no tiny text
- centered lime RS mark
- black/deep-green background
- subtle glow inside the icon mask
